Power Technology

How does it change in outer space?

We developed a lot of technology on earth before we were able to travel outside of the planet, and one of the most important of these is solar power collection. While it may have been something on earth that wasn't a necessity, out here in space it will be the biggest form of energy because of how easy it is to collect energy. Another system for energy collection that has some more risk factors is collecting energy from atom decay, otherwise known as nuclear power.
