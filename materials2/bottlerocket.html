Bottle Rocket Medals

For Ms. Reynolds Physics' class, Materials and Processing 2 was challenged with designing medals for the winners of Ms. Reynolds bottle rocket challenge. To help coordinate (for use of the glowforge) I was challenged with directing this project, and overseeing it. I learned how to communicate with students, and how to troubleshoot any of their issues.

(I also participated in the bottle rocket challenge... I did not win unfortunately)
